HORROR TOP TRUMPS

Welcome to top trumps a simple game to learn but careful you don't lose your head !


When you click the button to play you will be take to your final resting place to play for your life...


You will be dealt 20 cards from a pack of 40, each representing the most famous and glorious ghoulish fiends to ever grace the large and small screen


Both you and your opponent will be dealt 1 card per round, as the player you will select a category you feel your character excels in.


On doing so your opponents card will be revealed and the winner will take both cards to their score pile (a draw sees them shared)

This continues until one of you meets your fate as the loser and one stands victorious...


Enjoy and keep repeating... it's only a game, it's only a game...
