HORROR TOP TRUMPS


Welcome to 'HORROR TOP TRUMPS' a simple game to learn !


When you click the button to play you will be taken to your final resting place to play for your life...


RULES


You will be dealt 20 cards from a pack of 40, each representing the most famous ghoulish fiends to ever grace the silver screen


Both you and your opponent will be dealt 1 card per round, as the player you will select a category you feel your character excels in.

The winner takes both cards to their score pile (a draw sees them shared)


You will be told who won and then asked to deal the next card by clicking the prompt.


This continues until one of you reaaches an unassailable score of 21!


Just keep repeating... it's only a game, it's only a game...
